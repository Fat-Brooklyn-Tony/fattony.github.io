    Week 12: Pair Trading

    Kundan Kishore’s Webinar on Pair Trading

    • Risk Appetite determines the capital to invest in trading.
    • Technical Analysis theories only provide and focus on affirming examples.
      • Non-Sucker: Find disproving examples for every theory thrown at you.
    • Trading requires pairing strategies and mastering one.
    • Market neutral strategy - traders should gain from volatility of the market rather than how the market moves.
    • Options and Pairs are market neutral strategies.
    • Arbitrage
      • Statistical arbitrage - used in pair trading
        • Correlation between stocks
    • Demo
      • Closing data of HDFC and Kotak over 1 year
      • Pair trade between stocks with correlation > 0.9
      • Take a call on the correlation threshold.
        • Can depend on the sector
      • Backtest the correlation
      • Find correlation metric within a sector.
    • Pair trading
      • Market neutral strategy.
      • Find correlation coefficient between 2 stocks.
      • Consider the sector risk.
        • Selected the pair stocks in the same/similar sector.
      • Stocks should have similar market capitalization. (large cap)
      • Mean reverting theory
    • Power is cut at the 30th minute.
    • “The best would be to wait a second.”
