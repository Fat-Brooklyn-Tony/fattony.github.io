    Week 08: Annual Reports

    How to Read the Annual Report of a Company

    • Annual Report presents all the financial information about the company over the last year.
    • The AR is also the best source to get information related to the qualitative aspects of the company.
    • Comparing ARs of the companies within the same industry can present insight into how the company is run - whether it is worthwhile to invest.
    • Reading many ARs of companies in the same industry can be used to get an idea about the industry in general.
    • Financial Statements:
      • Profit & Loss
      • Balance Sheet
      • Cash Flow
    • Financial Statements come in 2 forms:
      • Standalone - independently as the company itself.
      • Consolidated - accounts for subsidiaries and holdings.
