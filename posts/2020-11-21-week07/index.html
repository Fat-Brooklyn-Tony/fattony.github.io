    Mindset of an Investor

    • Diff b/w Speculator, Trader and Investor
      • Speculator: speculates/opines about the uncertain events
      • Trader: focuses on the strategies to gain advantage from uncertain events (irrespective of market movement).
      • Investor: focuses on the long-term game, rather than short-term.
    • Investable Grade Attribute: traits that distinguish wealth making companies from wealth destroying companies.
      • Qualitative
      • Quantitative
