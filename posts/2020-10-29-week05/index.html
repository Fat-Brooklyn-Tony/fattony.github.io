    The Trading Terminal

      • CNC - Long position

    Clearance and Settlements

    • Trading day is T day.
    • All equity/stock settlements happen in India in T+2.
    • Brokerage stores the transaction money until clearance.

    Corporate Actions

    Dividends

    • Dividend is the reward distributed to the shareholders from the company.
    • Dividend Process:
      • Dividend Declaration Date
      • EX-Date
      • Record Date
      • Dividend Payout Date
    • Cum Dividend
    • Shorting on the cum dividend stock results in no gain.
    • Shorting results in stock square off.
    • Getting dividends results in holding the stock.

    Bonus Issue & Stock Split
