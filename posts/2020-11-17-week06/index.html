    Week 06: Key Market Events

    Key Events

    1. Monetary Policy
      • RBI sets the Monetary Policy.
      • 3 interest rates
        1. Repo Rate: RBI -> other banks
        2. Reverse Repo Rate: banks -> RBI
        3. CRR: cash reserve kept with the RBI
      • This event affects the interest-based market sectors - banks, automobile, housing finance, real estate, metals, etc.
    2. Inflation
      • Inflation indicates a sustained change in price of a commodity.
      • Measured by 2 indices
        1. Wholesale Price Index
        2. Consumer Price Index
      • Low interest rates -> high inflation
    3. Index of Industrial Production
      • Indicates the health of an industrial sector.
      • Lower IIP pressures RBI to lower interest rates.
    4. PMI
    5. Financial Budget
    6. Corporate Earnings Announcement
      • Released quarterly
      • Market reacts to the announcement with reference to the street expectations.
