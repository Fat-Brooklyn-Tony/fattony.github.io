    Week 04: Market Index and Shorting

    The Stock Market Index

    • Active Indices in Indian Markets: NIFTY, SENSEX
    • Free float market capitalization is used in Indian markets to calculate market indices.
      • We can come up with a different logic to calculate our own stock index.

    Takeaways

    • Index reflect mood of the market and national economy.
    • Index is useful for benchmarking against market performance.
    • Index trading is only possible through Derivative markets.

    Commonly Used Jargons

    • Bullish/Bearish Market
    • Face Value of Stock
    • OHLC
    • Lower/Upper Circuit
    • Long Position: buy -> sell (bullish)
    • Short Position: sell -> buy (bearish)
      • Example and intuition in the chapter.
    • Volumes
    • Market Segments
      • Capital Market: tradable securities
      • Futures and Options (Equity Derivatives Market): trade leveraged products
      • Wholesale Debt Market: fixed income securities
