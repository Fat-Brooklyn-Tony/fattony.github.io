    Week 11: Balance Sheet (Assets)

    Understanding the Balance Statement

    • Assets
      • Fixed Asset - assets that are consumed no longer than 365 days.
      • Current Asset - assets that are required for day-to-day operations.
    • Fixed Asset
      • Net Block = Gross Block - Accumulated Depreciation
      • CPIW - work that is not yet complete but capital expense is incurred -> moved to tangible asset.
    • Connecting the PL with the Balance Sheet
      • Depreciation in PL is for that year only while depreciation in Balance Sheet is accumulated.
