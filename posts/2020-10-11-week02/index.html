    Week 02: Basics of Raising Capital

    Financial Intermediaries

    • There are transaction charges (~Rs. 16) on DEMAT account in addition to the brokerage.

    Takeaways

    • DEMAT account acts like a digital vault for storing shares owned by you.
    • One needs a Trading account with the stock broker to trade stocks.
    • Depository (NSDL, CDSL) manages DEMAT account.

    Standard Process For Raising Business Capital

    • Seed Funding is the initial capital put together by the Promoter (entrepreneur) and the Angel Investors.
    • At this point of time, the company’s valuation is the Seed Fund itself.
    • Angel Investors provide funding on a lower investment scale at the initial stages while taking the most risk.
    • Venture Capital investors come in next with Series A funding.
    • Venture Capitalists provide funding on a medium investment scale and for further funding, the company should approach Private Equity investors.
    • Private Equity investors fund on a higher investment scale and likely install members on the company’s board to navigate the company in their desired direction.
    • The valuation of the company changes after each funding round - creating wealth in process.
    • IPO is the next step to raise further capital.
    • Pvt. Ltd companies can be treated as an independent financial entity (from the owner/promoter) with a separate bank account.
