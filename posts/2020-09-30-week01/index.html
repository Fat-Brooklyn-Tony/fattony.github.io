    Week 01: Why should one invest?

    To kick things off, we start at the most basic, fundamental and significant questions of them all.

    1. Why should I invest?

    • Fight Inflation - saving won’t last after retirement.
    • Investment can be thought of as a source of passive income.

    2. What are the ways to invest?

    The following 4 asset class are the way to go depending on one’s appetite for risk and expected returns:

    1. Fixed income instruments
    2. Equity
    3. Real estate
    4. Commodities (precious metals)

    Takeaways

    • Invest to beat inflation and secure one’s future.
    • Risk and Return go hand in hand when considering an investment asset class.
    • Equities/stocks are the preferred investment asset class to beat inflation in the long run.
    • Stock Market : Local Market :: Stock Exchange : Shop in the market.
    • Debentures as a way for corporates to procure funds indirectly from the public.
    • SEBI regulates the entire Indian stock market.
    • Open a DEMAT account in order to trade in stocks.
